Welcome to Door Too Door

Introduction:

Here at Door Too Door, we strive for perfection or at the very least, a strong sense of character. Our doors are stol... salvaged... from the most presteen and storied places this broad and noble land has to offer. From lonesome prairie homesteads to soot-kissed train stations and backroom speakeasies that may or may not still exist on record, we bring these proud wooden slabs to every corner of this here wide world.


We ain’t just merchants we’re matchmakers. Each door’s got a tale carved deep in its grain: some whisper, some creak, some holler outright. Whether from a soot-stained chapel, a rickety boarding house, or the pantry of a now condemned pie shop, every one of our offerings is pre-worn, pre-loved, and pre-sumed legal.


We're your salesman, and you're our precious custom'er. You might say we’re in the business of sending old doors off to greener hinges. We find ’em, clean ’em up just enough, slap some dignity back on, and see them off on their next journey with or without original hardware.


Our doors are always on vacation and receive regular recreational pedicures strictly for morale and stackability. It’s our special way of maintaining both structural integrity and workplace camaraderie.


Please, no further investigation into company practices is needed due to a currently ongoing and entirely misunderstood FBI matter. We thank you kindly for your patience, discretion, and continued interest in our most noble wares.
